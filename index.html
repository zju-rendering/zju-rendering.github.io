About me

I am an Assistant Research Professor at State Key Laboratory of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University. Before joining ZJU in 2021,I was a postdoctoral researcher at KAIST,advised by Sung-Eui Yoon. Earlier,I received my Ph.D. degree from the CAD&CG State Key Lab of Zhejiang University,advised by Prof. Hujun Bao and Prof. Rui Wang.
My recent research interests include neural rendering, 3D reconstruction, and optical neural networks.
